Our Story

During our university days, we often found ourselves experimenting in the kitchen, trying out new recipes, and exploring different cuisines. However, we faced challenges in finding reliable and user-friendly resources that catered to our specific needs and dietary preferences. That's when the idea for Master Chef was born.
We envisioned a platform that would not only provide a vast collection of recipes but also empower users to customize their cooking experience based on the ingredients they have on hand. With Master Chef, we aim to eliminate the frustration of searching for recipes that match your available ingredients and dietary restrictions.
